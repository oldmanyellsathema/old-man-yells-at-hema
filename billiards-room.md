Past the library, behind the curtain, there's a room, a room with bad lighting and sharp tools.

---

### ⚔ Scouting Diary & Combat Judge

A tracking tool for HEMA. Built because nothing else existed that wasn't a spreadsheet held together with spite. It's in progress so, relax.

**What it does:**

- **Log hits** During sparring. attacker, defender, attack type, target zone, points. Track yourself or scout anyone against anyone from the sideline.
- **Fighter profiles** Heat maps showing where someone gets hit, what attacks they favor, their tendencies over time. Study your opponents. Study yourself.
- **Personal diary** Your weaknesses, your strengths, your patterns. The honest version.
- **Live judging** Two body figures (Red & Gold), tap to score. Clock with count up or down. Afterblow support. Score limits. Round robin pool generation with balanced scheduling.
- **Configurable rulesets** Set your own attack types, point matrix per attack × zone. Save presets for different weapons or tournament rules.
- **Export everything** — JSON, CSV. Hand the results file to the table captain and walk away.

All data stays on your device. No accounts, no server, no nonsense. Install it on your phone and it works offline.
